The Heart of It All

You're not just hiring a co-host.
You're joining a community.

Co-Hosting with Heart was built on a simple belief: hosts are stronger together. Our network is a warm, collaborative circle of Airbnb hosts and co-hosts across Austin and beyond — people who share insights, coordinate resources, and genuinely cheer each other's success.

When you bring us on, you gain access to that entire ecosystem. The collective experience of our network means your listing benefits from strategies that have been tested, refined, and proven — not just guessed at.

What We Handle

Everything that keeps
you up at night — handled.

01

Dynamic Pricing & Revenue Strategy

We manage PriceLabs daily — monitoring demand shifts, local events, seasonal patterns, and competitor rates to keep your nightly price optimized at all times. No guessing. No leaving money on the table.

02

Full Guest Relations & Support

Every guest call, message, complaint, and special request comes to us — not you. We take complete ownership of the guest experience, protecting your reviews and your peace of mind simultaneously.

03

Airbnb Insurance & Claims Management

When damage happens, the AirCover process can be exhausting. We handle the entire claims journey — documentation, submission, follow-up, and resolution — so you're protected without the bureaucratic headache.

04

City Compliance & STR Licensing

Austin's short-term rental regulations evolve constantly. We ensure your property stays fully up-to-code — permits, safety requirements, noise ordinances, and any changes to local STR laws — before they become your problem.

Our Story

A ground-up business
built on genuine care.

Co-Hosting with Heart started in Austin, TX — a city that takes hospitality seriously. We built this business because we saw hosts drowning in the operational side of their listings: pricing anxiety, difficult guests, confusing compliance rules, and insurance claims that felt impossible.

Our answer was a co-hosting model grounded in community. We brought together a network of experienced hosts and co-hosts who share resources, insights, and a genuine commitment to each other's success. When you join us, you're not an account — you're part of that circle.

And because we run on a performance basis, we have every reason in the world to pour our hearts into your listing. When you win, we win.
